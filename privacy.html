Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

iClicker AutoClicker ("the extension") is a Chrome browser extension that records and replays click sequences on webpages. This policy explains how the extension handles data.

Data We Collect

The extension does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data stays on your device.

Local Storage

The extension stores your recorded click sequences (screen coordinates and element labels) locally in Chrome's storage.local API. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is never sent to any server
  • Can be cleared at any time using the "Clear All Steps" button

Permissions Used

  • activeTab — to interact with the current browser tab when you click the extension icon
  • scripting — to inject the click-recording and playback script into the page
  • storage — to save your recorded click sequence locally between sessions

No permission is used to access browsing history, passwords, or any personal information.

Third Parties

The extension does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising.
